Preparations Before vaccine

The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the
strength of body's immune system. therefore, there are
several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19
vaccine work:

  • Avoid alcoholic Beverages

    We implore you to consume any type of alcoholic beverage
    to avoid the probability any type of secondary effect.

  • Avoid stress

    Stress has a profound effect on immune work.
    Moreover,porlonged stress can increase cortisol
    production and oxidative stress in the body.

  • Eat healthy food

    It is necessary to control your diet before
    and after the vaccine, such as not eating too much,
    eating balanced meals.

  • Get enough sleep

    Getting plenty of rest helps your immune system function at its full potential.
    To improve the quality of your sleep before you get vaccinated,
    be strategic about what you eat, especially for dinner.

  • Avoid stress

    stress, depression and unhealthy behaviors can negatively affect the body's
    immune response to vaccination so we ask
    that you remain as calm as possible.
